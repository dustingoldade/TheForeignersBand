The Foreigners Band

Who Are We?

We are The Foreigners Band, a local phenomenon that have been ravaging the Rock Scene in Krakow. Since the band's debut in 2017, we have performed over 200 live shows, recorded an original album, and made several music videos.

As the name suggests, we are musicians from all around the world, but we live in Poland and play your favorite music as well as our own original music.
